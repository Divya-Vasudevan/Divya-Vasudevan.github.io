Vurve Website

UI / UX Redesign Concept, Solo Project


This is a redesign concept for the website of Vurve salon, Chennai. The redesign was done to improve the UX of the website and also to seamlessly showcase the brand and what it stands for.


Tools: Axure RP, Illustrator, Affinity Photo

UI Screens

Vurve is a popular salon that has wonderful customer service. They also portray themselves as a high end brand and the UI was changed to reflect their qualities.
