ui/ux design + axure prototyping + social media

“identify opportunities for improvement in Quora and propose workable solutions”



duration

15 weeks


solo project

research, ideation, and design concept development

approach

identifying user groups and roles.

I started my process by researching the different kinds of people using Quora and created a process map depicting the significant roles and the way they use the app.

personas

usage scenarios.

Speaking to different people about why they used Quora and looking into online forums and discussions about the app helped me create several personas. These personas helped to create an image of the scenarios in which the app will be used.

Highlighting the various roles Quora plays, I created a word cloud to help me identify the opportunities that would be appreciated by users.

opportunities identified

using what they already have and adding a little more.

Missed opportunities include:
1. Poorly accessible blogging functionality
2. Irrelevant feed content

Additional opportunities include:
1. Allowing saving of essential content and note-taking
2. Improving how related content can be discovered

Based on the identified opportunities prototypes were created.

final design

redesigned feed.

Separated sponsored and suggested content to allow users to read based on interest. Content shown will be based on history and liked topics and questions.

filter content.

Topics can now be filtered further based on specific interests allowing users to read more relevant content.

related topics.

New representation for related topics easily allows users to identify possible relevant information more easily.

blog accessibility and search suggest.

Blog content is made accessible from multiple loctions - top bar in feed, search and from within topics. Also improved search to show content suggested in different categories.

new actions.

Can browse through related questions based on current and previous searches without having to scroll all the way to the bottom. Can also take notes and highlight content for later use.

notes.

Complete view of all the notes and highlights created to quickly scan through important content.
