about me.

I am an interaction designer from India, currently pursuing my masters in Interaction Design at the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, Michigan. I also have a background in information technology, specifically in backend software development.

what I did.

I did my bachelor's in Information and Communication Technology, following which, I worked as a backend developer at ZOHO corporation for two years. Though the work there was challenging and in line with my interests, I found myself unhappy with how far removed from the users it was. This led me to discover interaction design.

Following a period of self-learning, I did two internships. The first was in Chennai with Tablo Noir where I focussed mostly on graphic design and the other was at BYG in Bangalore, where I was involved in graphic design and web design and development. My experiences there led me to want to learn and explore more, leading me to an MFA in CCS.

my life outside design.

Stories are my passion, and I have pursued them through reading and classical dance. You can frequently find me holed up in a corner blissfully reading a novel. Dance, on the other hand, taught me about various stories from Indian mythology and provided a creative outlet for me.

about this site.

Though programming is not my life anymore, I continue to experiment and learn with a focus on design elements instead of engineering ones. This portfolio site is the last, best, and only completely working website of seven experiments in learning and exploring HTML and CSS animations in web design.
